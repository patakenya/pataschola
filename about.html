Our Mission

Pata Scholarship exists to bridge the gap between talented Kenyan students and educational opportunities. We believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to quality education.

Through our innovative platform, we connect students from all backgrounds with thousands of scholarship opportunities, making the application process simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

"Every student deserves the chance to pursue their dreams. We're here to make that possible."

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading scholarship platform, enabling every talented student to access quality education regardless of their financial background.

Our Goal

Support 100,000 students by 2030 through scholarship opportunities, mentorship programs, and educational resources.

Our Story

How we started and where we're going

2020

The Beginning

Pata Scholarship was founded by a group of Kenyan educators and tech entrepreneurs who witnessed firsthand how talented students were missing out on scholarship opportunities due to lack of information and complicated application processes.

2021

Platform Launch

We launched our digital platform, making it easier for students to discover and apply for scholarships. In our first year, we helped 1,200 students secure over KES 150 million in scholarships.

2023

Rapid Growth

We expanded our partnerships to include over 30 scholarship providers and introduced features like application tracking, document management, and personalized scholarship recommendations.

2026

Today & Beyond

We're proud to serve over 12,500 students across all 47 counties in Kenya. Our platform now features 50+ scholarship opportunities, and we're expanding to include mentorship programs and career guidance services.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Accessibility

We believe education opportunities should be accessible to all students, regardless of their location or background. Our platform is designed to be simple and user-friendly.

Transparency

We maintain complete transparency in our processes, from scholarship listings to application status. Students always know where they stand.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from our technology platform to our customer support, ensuring the best experience for our users.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to improve our platform and services, leveraging technology to make scholarship applications easier and more efficient.

Integrity

We operate with the highest level of integrity, ensuring that all scholarships listed on our platform are legitimate and that student data is protected.

Empowerment

We empower students to take control of their educational future by providing them with the tools, resources, and support they need to succeed.
